I'm Alex –
Web Designer + Developer + Artist.


Web Developer, Art Director, Graphic Designer, and Artist residing Dallas, Texas. I graduated from Texas Christian University in Fort Worth Texas with a BFA in Graphic Design in 2010. My work derives from found objects, DIY aesthetic, trash art, and creative collaboration, which is relevant within my design and web projects. I am currently enrolled in an intensive Web Development Bootcamp at Southern Methodist University.
